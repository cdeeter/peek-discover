How It Works


Discovering new things to do in your city has never been easier! Peek, the one stop shop for tours and activities,

Step 1

Create your profile

Set up activity preferences and account information, get prepped for new adventures, and more.

Step 3

Explore your city!

We make it easy for you to find and experience the hidden gems of your city!
